About Yushi Zhang

I combine rigorous academic training from Yale (MPH in Social & Behavioral Sciences) and Harvard research experience with proven real-world impact. As founder of China's first grassroots organization for women's reproductive health rights, I've driven systemic change—increasing breastfeeding rates from 3% to 28% and creating over 1,000 jobs in the reproductive health sector.

My approach starts with one person, one action. Whether you're a woman leader seeking to overcome workplace barriers or an organization designing women's initiatives, I provide behavioral science-backed solutions that create lasting change.
